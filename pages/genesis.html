My Favorite Genesis Vehicles

2024 Genesis G90

While it is certainly the priciest of the Genesis sedans, you certainly get what you pay for. A fast car with amenities that'll wow everyone. Best of all, the interior design is impeccable.

2024 Genesis G80

The younger sibling of the big bad G90 still has much to offer, and for a starting MSRP of roughly $55,000. Keeping most of the glam that is Genesis and with an electrified option? Yes please.

2024+ Genesis Magma Concept Cars

So this isn't much a specific car, but rather a genre of cars that Genesis is working on. They're not for sale and it's unsure which models will recieve this conceptual treatment, but the color orange with sporty designs has me intrigued!
