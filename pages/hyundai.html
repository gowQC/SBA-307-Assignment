My Favorite Hyundai Vehicles

2024 Hyundai Sonata N-Line

Easily my favorite car under $40,000 MSRP. With this trim, it has a bit of everything: speed, style, MPG, and all at a price well worth the money.

2023-2024 IONIQ 6

One of my picks if I had to go all electric. The design is unique, the specifications are great, and the idea of not having to spend money on gas again is something I would consider.

2023-2024 Hyundai Tucson

Not really much of an SUV driver, but... this things speaks to me. Its stylish design alone piques my curiosity with driving SUV's (Hyundai just knows how to make good body lines).
